ABOUT US

About KennyKraft Cabinetry Supply

KennyKraft Cabinetry Supply is built on a simple principle: great cabinetry begins with accurate design, clear communication, and real-world experience. For over 20 years, we’ve delivered cabinetry solutions across the United States that balance design intent with practical execution.

Our background includes working for and alongside cabinetry manufacturers, contractors, and real estate professionals—from fully custom, made-in-the-USA lines to high-quality imported systems. This experience allows us to create designs that are visually refined, technically sound, and ready to build.

Based in the Midwest, we support projects throughout the Chicago metro area, Madison, and Milwaukee, oƯering on-site measurements, custom cabinet design, and cabinetry supply. We also provide professional remote cabinet design services for projects nationwide, tailored to manufacturer specifications and project requirements.

Whether working with homeowners, contractors, or investors, our focus remains the same: precise planning, honest guidance, and attention to detail at every stage. At KennyKraft Cabinetry Supply, we don’t just design cabinets—we help bring projects together.
